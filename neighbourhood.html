WHAT'S AROUND?

DISCOVER THE
NEIGHBOURHOOD

Positioned in one of Ottawa's most walkable and dynamic communities, 82 Genest St offers unmatched connectivity and urban convenience.

Beechwood Avenue is lined with independent restaurants, cafés, and bars — all within a short walk. From casual breakfast spots to local craft beer and wine bars, the neighbourhood's food scene is one of Ottawa's most well-rounded outside of the Glebe.
The Ottawa River Pathway runs just minutes away, offering one of the city's best multi-use trails for running and cycling. The Rideau River Eastern Pathway is equally accessible, connecting you to kilometres of waterfront trail in both directions.
Riverain Park sits nearby along the Ottawa River, offering open green space, a beach, and stunning views. Beechwood Cemetery — a National Historic Site — provides a peaceful 160-acre green space ideal for walks year-round.
OC Transpo buses run along Beechwood Avenue with frequent service to downtown Ottawa. The neighbourhood is also extremely bikeable, with dedicated cycling infrastructure and a 99 Bike Score — most daily errands can be done without a car.
Beechwood Avenue's commercial strip has everything you need day-to-day — grocery, pharmacy, boutique retail, and personal services — without the noise of a big-box corridor. The Rideau Centre and Byward Market are a short bus or bike ride away.
